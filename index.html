Biography

Keyu Zhu is a first-year graduate student at H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering, Georgia Institute of Technology. Prior to coming to Georgia Tech, he graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China with B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics.

His current research interest lies in the intersection of machine learning and optimization, especially in the application to online decision-making. Besides, he works on algorithmic game theory, which connects theoretical computer science and microeconomics.
